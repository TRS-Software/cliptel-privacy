1. Overview

ClipTel is a Chrome browser extension that lets you transfer clipboard contents between your PC and your mobile phone using your own private Telegram bot. The extension is designed with privacy in mind: no ClipTel server is involved in transmitting your data.

2. Data We Collect and Store

2.1 Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored exclusively in chrome.storage.local on your device:

DataPurpose
Telegram bot tokenAuthenticates with the Telegram Bot API
Telegram chat IDIdentifies your private chat channel
Last update IDTracks which messages have been processed
Poll interval settingYour preferred background check interval
Clipboard history (last 50)Shows sent/received snippets in the popup

This data never leaves your device to any ClipTel server. It is deleted if you uninstall the extension or use "Disconnect & reset" in settings.

2.2 Data Transmitted to Telegram

When you send or receive text through ClipTel, the content is transmitted to Telegram Messenger Inc. via their Bot API (api.telegram.org). This is TLS-encrypted but not end-to-end encrypted. Your IP address is visible to Telegram's servers. Telegram's Privacy Policy applies: telegram.org/privacy.

3. Third Parties

ClipTel only communicates with one external service:

Telegram Messenger Inc.
71–75 Shelton Street, London, WC2H 9JQ, United Kingdom
Privacy Policy

No analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services are used.

5. Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the EEA, you have the right to access, delete, and port your data. Since all data is stored locally, you can view it in Chrome DevTools → Application → Local Storage, and delete it via "Disconnect & reset" or by uninstalling the extension. To lodge a complaint, contact your national data protection authority.

6. Security

  • Bot token stored in chrome.storage.local, inaccessible to web pages
  • Token never logged to browser console or shown in notifications
  • All Telegram API calls use HTTPS
  • Host permissions strictly limited to api.telegram.org

7. Children

ClipTel is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.
